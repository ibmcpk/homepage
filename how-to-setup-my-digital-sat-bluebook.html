How to setup my digital SAT bluebook

team July 13, 2025
Before You Start

System Requirements Check

  • Test your internet connection (stable connection required for initial setup)

  • Close all unnecessary applications to avoid distractions

  • Charge your device fully or keep it plugged in during the test

Physical Setup

  • Find a quiet, well-lit space where you won't be interrupted

  • Use a comfortable chair and desk setup

  • Have scratch paper and pencils ready (you can use these during the actual test)

  • Turn off phone notifications and put devices in airplane mode

 
Step-by-Step Instructions

 
1. Download and Install Bluebook

  • Go to the official College Board website: https://bluebook.app.collegeboard.org/

  • Download the Bluebook app for your operating system (Windows, Mac, iPad, or Chromebook)

  • Install the application following the provided instructions

  • Create or log into your College Board account

 
2. Set Up Your Test Environment

  • Launch the Bluebook app and sign in with your College Board credentials

  • Navigate to the practice test section

  • Select "Full-Length Practice Test" for your first diagnostic

  • Choose a time when you can dedicate 2 hours and 14 minutes without interruptions

 
3. Take the Diagnostic Test

Test Timing and Format:

  • Total test time: 2 hours 14 minutes

  • Reading and Writing: 64 minutes (2 modules, 32 minutes each)

  • Math: 70 minutes (2 modules, 35 minutes each)

  • 10-minute break between sections

  • Built-in graphing calculator available for entire Math section

Begin the test:

  • Start the test and treat it exactly like the real exam

  • Each section is adaptive, meaning question difficulty adjusts based on your performance

  • Use the built-in tools: highlighter, answer eliminator, and reference sheet for math

  • Don't pause or take breaks beyond the scheduled 10-minute break between sections
